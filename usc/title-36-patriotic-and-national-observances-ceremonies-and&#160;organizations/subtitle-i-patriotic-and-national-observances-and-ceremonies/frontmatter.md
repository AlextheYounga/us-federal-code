Subtitle I—Patriotic and National Observances and Ceremonies
----------

#### Amendments ####

**2014**—Pub. L. 113–237, §3(a)(2)(A), Dec. 18, 2014, 128 Stat. 2835, struck out items for part A "Observances and Ceremonies", which consisted of items for chapters 1 to 9, and for part B "United States Government Organizations Involved With Observances and Ceremonies", which consisted of items for chapters 21 to 25.

**2004**—Pub. L. 108–447, div. J, title I, §109(b)(1), Dec. 8, 2004, 118 Stat. 3344, substituted "March, and Tree" for ", and March" in item for chapter 3.
