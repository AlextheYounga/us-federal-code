### §70907. Exclusive right to name,<sup><a href="#70906_1_target" name="70906_1">1</a></sup> seals, emblems, and badges ###

The FFA and its authorized chapters and associations of chapters have the exclusive right to use the names "Future Farmers of America" and "National FFA Organization," <sup><a href="#70907_2_target" name="70907_2">2</a></sup> and the initials FFA as representing an agricultural education membership organization and seals, emblems, and badges the FFA adopts.

(Pub. L. 105–225, Aug. 12, 1998, 112 Stat. 1358; Pub. L. 116–7, §8, Feb. 21, 2019, 133 Stat. 481.)
