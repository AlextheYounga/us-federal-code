### Part I—Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Part J of title IV of Pub. L. 89–329, comprising this part, was redesignated part F of title III of Pub. L. 89–329 by Pub. L. 110–315, title III, §316(a)(2), Aug. 14, 2008, 122 Stat. 3185, and transferred to part F (§1067q) of subchapter III of this chapter.
