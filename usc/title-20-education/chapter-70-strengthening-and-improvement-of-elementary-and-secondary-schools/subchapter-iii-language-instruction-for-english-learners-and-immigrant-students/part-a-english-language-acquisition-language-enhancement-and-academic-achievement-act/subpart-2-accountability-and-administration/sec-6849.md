### §6849. Prohibition ###

In carrying out this part, the Secretary shall neither mandate nor preclude the use of a particular curricular or pedagogical approach to educating English learners.

(Pub. L. 89–10, title III, §3128, formerly §3129, as added Pub. L. 107–110, title III, §301, Jan. 8, 2002, 115 Stat. 1706; renumbered §3128 and amended Pub. L. 114–95, title III, §§3001(2)(B), 3003(l), Dec. 10, 2015, 129 Stat. 1953, 1964.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior section 3128 of Pub. L. 89–10 was renumbered section 3127 and is classified to section 6848 of this title.

#### Amendments ####

**2015**—Pub. L. 114–95, §3003(l), substituted "English learners" for "limited English proficient children".

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries** ####
