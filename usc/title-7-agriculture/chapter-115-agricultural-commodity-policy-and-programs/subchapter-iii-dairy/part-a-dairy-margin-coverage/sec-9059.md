### §9059. Duration ###

Dairy margin coverage shall end on December 31, 2025.

(Pub. L. 113–79, title I, §1409, Feb. 7, 2014, 128 Stat. 693; Pub. L. 115–334, title I, §1401(l), Dec. 20, 2018, 132 Stat. 4518; Pub. L. 118–22, div. B, title I, §102(c)(2)(B)(i), Nov. 17, 2023, 137 Stat. 115; Pub. L. 118–158, div. D, §4101(c)(4)(A)(i), Dec. 21, 2024, 138 Stat. 1768.)
