### §4362. Application to Canada and Mexico ###

Pursuant to article 1902 of the North American Free Trade Agreement and section 3438 <sup><a href="#4362_1_target" name="4362_1">1</a></sup> of this title, this subchapter and the amendments made by this title <sup><a href="#4362_1_target" name="4362_1">1</a></sup> shall apply with respect to goods from Canada and Mexico.

(Pub. L. 114–125, title IV, §403, Feb. 24, 2016, 130 Stat. 156.)

#### References in Text ####

Section 3438 of this title, referred to in text, was formerly section 408 of Pub. L. 103–182 and was renumbered as section 418 of Pub. L. 116–113 and amended by Pub. L. 116–113, title V, §504(i), Jan. 29, 2020, 134 Stat. 76, and transferred to section 4588 of this title, effective on the date on which the USMCA entered into force (July 1, 2020). As amended, section 4588 of this title no longer relates to the North American Free Trade Agreement.

This subchapter, referred to in text, was in the original "this title", meaning title IV of Pub. L. 114–125, Feb. 24, 2016, 130 Stat. 155, which is classified principally to this subchapter. For complete classification of title IV to the Code, see section 401 of Pub. L. 114–125, set out as a Short Title note under section 4301 of this title and Tables.

The amendments made by this title, referred to in text, mean the amendments made by title IV of Pub. L. 114–125, Feb. 24, 2016, 130 Stat. 155. For complete classification of title IV to the Code, see section 401 of Pub. L. 114–125, set out as a Short Title note under section 4301 of this title and Tables.
