### §410w. Administration, protection, and development ###

When established pursuant to this subchapter, the park shall be administered, protected, and developed by the Secretary of the Interior in accordance with the provisions of sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 <sup><a href="#410w_1_target" name="410w_1">1</a></sup> of this title, as amended and supplemented, and sections 461 to 467 <sup><a href="#410w_1_target" name="410w_1">1</a></sup> of this title.

(Pub. L. 86–321, §5, Sept. 21, 1959, 73 Stat. 592.)

#### References in Text ####

Sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by Pub. L. 113–287, §7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272. For disposition of sections, see Disposition Table preceding section 100101 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs.

Sections 461 to 467 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by Pub. L. 113–287, §7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272. For disposition of sections, see Disposition Table preceding section 100101 of Title 54.
