### §459a–6. Acquisition of non-Federal land within boundaries of recreational area ###

Section 452a <sup><a href="#459a-6_1_target" name="459a-6_1">1</a></sup> of this title is amended to extend the authority of the Secretary of the Interior, contained therein, to the Cape Hatteras National Seashore Recreational Area.

(Aug. 6, 1956, ch. 988, §1, 70 Stat. 1066.)

#### References in Text ####

Section 452a of this title, referred to in text, was in the original "the Act of August 31, 1954 (68 Stat. 1037)," meaning act Aug. 31, 1954, ch. 1163, 68 Stat. 1037, which was repealed by Pub. L. 113–287, §7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272. See section 101102 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs.
