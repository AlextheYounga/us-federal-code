### §410uu–2. Cooperative agreements ###

The Secretary may enter into a cooperative agreement with the Ohio Historical Society, the Archeological <sup><a href="#410uu-2_1_target" name="410uu-2_1">1</a></sup> Conservancy, and other public and private entities for consultation and assistance in the interpretation and management of the park.

(Pub. L. 102–294, §3, May 27, 1992, 106 Stat. 186.)

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be "Archaeological".](#410uu-2_1)
