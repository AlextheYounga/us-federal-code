### §429a–2. Change in name to Tupelo National Battlefield; administration ###

The Tupelo National Battlefield site is redesignated the Tupelo National Battlefield which shall continue to be administered pursuant to sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 <sup><a href="#429a-2_1_target" name="429a-2_1">1</a></sup> of this title, as amended and supplemented.

(Pub. L. 87–133, §2, Aug. 10, 1961, 75 Stat. 336.)

#### References in Text ####

Sections 1, 2, 3, and 4 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by Pub. L. 113–287, §7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3272. For disposition of sections, see Disposition Table preceding section 100101 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs.
