### §460nnn–2. Valid existing rights ###

Nothing in this subchapter shall effect <sup><a href="#460nnn-2_1_target" name="460nnn-2_1">1</a></sup> any valid existing right.

(Pub. L. 106–399, §4, Oct. 30, 2000, 114 Stat. 1658.)

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be “affect”.](#460nnn-2_1)
