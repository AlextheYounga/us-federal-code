### §17*o*. Repealed or Omitted ###

#### Codification ####

Section, Pub. L. 104–333, div. I, title VIII, §814(a), Nov. 12, 1996, 110 Stat. 4190; Pub. L. 106–176, title I, §120(a)(1), Mar. 10, 2000, 114 Stat. 28, which related to National Park Service housing improvement, was repealed in part and omitted in part.

Pars. (2) to (19) were repealed, and pars. (2), (3), (6) to (14), and (17) to (19) were also reenacted as subchapter III of chapter 1013 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs, by Pub. L. 113–287, §§3, 7, Dec. 19, 2014, 128 Stat. 3094, 3272, which Act enacted Title 54. For disposition of section 17o, see Disposition Table preceding section 100101 of Title 54.

Par. (1), which stated the purposes of the section, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 54.
