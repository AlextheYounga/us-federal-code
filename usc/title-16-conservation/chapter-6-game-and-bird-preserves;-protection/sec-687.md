### §687. Grand Canyon Game Preserve included in park ###

Such parts of the Grand Canyon National Game Preserve, designated under authority of section 684 of this title, as are by this Act included with <sup><a href="#687_1_target" name="687_1">1</a></sup> the Grand Canyon National Park are excluded and eliminated from said game preserve.

(Feb. 26, 1919, ch. 44, §9, 40 Stat. 1178.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### References in Text ####

This Act, referred to in text, is act Feb. 26, 1919, ch. 44, 40 Stat. 1175, which is classified principally to subchapter XXIV (§221 et seq.) of chapter 1 of this title. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see Tables.

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be "within".](#687_1)
