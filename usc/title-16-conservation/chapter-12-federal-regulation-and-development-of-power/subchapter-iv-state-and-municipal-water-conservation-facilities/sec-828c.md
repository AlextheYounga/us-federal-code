### ยง828c. Applicability of this subchapter ###

Except as herein provided, the provisions of this subchapter shall not be construed as repealing or affecting any of the provisions of the Federal Power Act [16 U.S.C. 791a et seq.].

(Aug. 15, 1953, ch. 503, ยง4, 67 Stat. 587.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### References in Text ####

The Federal Power Act, referred to in text, is act June 10, 1920, ch. 285, 41 Stat. 1063, which is classified generally to this chapter. For complete classification of this Act to the Code, see section 791a of this title and Tables.

#### Codification ####

Section was not enacted as part of the Federal Power Act which generally comprises this chapter.
