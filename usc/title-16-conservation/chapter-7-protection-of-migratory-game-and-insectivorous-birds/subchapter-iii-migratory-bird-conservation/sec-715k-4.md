### §715k–4. Accounting and use of appropriations ###

Funds appropriated each fiscal year pursuant to sections 715k–3 to 715k–5 of this title shall be accounted for, added to, and used for purposes of the migratory bird conservation fund <sup><a href="#715k-4_1_target" name="715k-4_1">1</a></sup> established pursuant to section 718d of this title.

(Pub. L. 87–383, §2, Oct. 4, 1961, 75 Stat. 813.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

Section was not enacted as part of the Migratory Bird Conservation Act which comprises this subchapter.

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be capitalized.](#715k-4_1)
