### §6405. Emergency assistance ###

The Administrator may make grants to any State, local, or territorial government agency with jurisdiction over coral reefs for emergencies to address unforeseen or disaster-related circumstance <sup><a href="#6405_1_target" name="6405_1">1</a></sup> pertaining to coral reefs or coral reef ecosystems.

(Pub. L. 106–562, title II, §206, Dec. 23, 2000, 114 Stat. 2804.)
