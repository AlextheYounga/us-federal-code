### §470. Transferred or Omitted ###

#### Codification ####

Section, Pub. L. 89–665, §1, Oct. 15, 1966, 80 Stat. 915; Pub. L. 96–515, title I, §101(a), Dec. 12, 1980, 94 Stat. 2987, related to short title, findings, and policy of National Historic Preservation Act. Subsec. (a), which provided short title for subchapter, was transferred and is set out as a note under section 100101 of Title 54, National Park Service and Related Programs. Subsec. (b), which related to findings, was omitted from the Code following the enactment of Title 54.
