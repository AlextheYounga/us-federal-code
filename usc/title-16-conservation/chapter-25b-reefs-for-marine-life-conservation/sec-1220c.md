### §1220c. Denial of applications; finality of decision ###

A decision by the Secretary denying any application for a <sup><a href="#1220c_1_target" name="1220c_1">1</a></sup> obsolete ship under this chapter is final.

(Pub. L. 92–402, §6, Aug. 22, 1972, 86 Stat. 618; Pub. L. 98–623, title II, §207(1), Nov. 8, 1984, 98 Stat. 3397.)

#### Amendments ####

**1984**—Pub. L. 98–623 substituted "obsolete ship" for "Liberty ship".

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be "an".](#1220c_1)
