### §568a. Cooperation by Secretary of Agriculture with Territories and other possessions ###

The Secretary of Agriculture is authorized to cooperate with Territories and other possessions of the United States on the same terms and conditions as with States under sections 566, 567,<sup><a href="#568a_1_target" name="568a_1">1</a></sup> and 568 of this title.

(Apr. 13, 1926, ch. 134, 44 Stat. 250.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### References in Text ####

Sections 566 and 567 of this title, referred to in text, were repealed by Pub. L. 95–313, §13(a)(1), July 1, 1978, 92 Stat. 374.
