### §8930. Rule of construction ###

Nothing in this chapter shall be construed as an authorization for the use of military force.

(Pub. L. 113–272, §11, Dec. 18, 2014, 128 Stat. 2966.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### References in Text ####

This chapter, referred to in text, was in the original "this Act or an amendment made by this Act", meaning Pub. L. 113–272, Dec. 18, 2014, 128 Stat. 2952, which enacted this chapter and provisions set out as a note under section 8921 of this title. Pub. L. 113–272 did not make any amendments.
