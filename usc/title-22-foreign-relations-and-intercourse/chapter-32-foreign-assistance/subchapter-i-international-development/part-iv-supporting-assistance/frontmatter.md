### Part IV—Supporting Assistance ###

#### **Statutory Notes and Related Subsidiaries** ####

#### References to Part IV of Subchapter I Deemed References to Part IV of Subchapter II ####

References to part IV of subchapter I of this chapter, or any sections thereof, are deemed references to part IV of subchapter II (§2346 et seq.) of this chapter, or to appropriate sections thereof. See section 202(b) of Pub. L. 92–226, set out as a note under section 2346 of this title.
