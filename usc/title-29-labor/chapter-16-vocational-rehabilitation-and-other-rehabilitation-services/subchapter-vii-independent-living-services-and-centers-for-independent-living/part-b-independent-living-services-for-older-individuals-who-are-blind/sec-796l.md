### §796*l*. Authorization of appropriations ###

There are authorized to be appropriated to carry out this part such sums as may be necessary for each of the fiscal years 1999 through 2003.

(Pub. L. 93–112, title VII, §753, as added Pub. L. 105–220, title IV, §410, Aug. 7, 1998, 112 Stat. 1241.)

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior section 796l, Pub. L. 93–112, title VII, §753, as added Pub. L. 102–569, title VII, §703(a), Oct. 29, 1992, 106 Stat. 4468, authorized appropriations, prior to the general amendment of this subchapter by Pub. L. 105–220.
