### §558. Authorization of appropriations ###

There are hereby authorized to be appropriated such sums,<sup><a href="#558_1_target" name="558_1">1</a></sup> as may be necessary to carry out the provisions and purposes of this chapter.

(Aug. 31, 1954, ch. 1156, §8, 68 Stat. 1011; Pub. L. 102–486, title XXV, §2504(d)(3), Oct. 24, 1992, 106 Stat. 3106.)

#### Amendments ####

**1992**—Pub. L. 102–486 struck out "not to exceed $500,000 annually," after "such sums,".

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. The comma probably should not appear.](#558_1)
