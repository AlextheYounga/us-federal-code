### §§405 to 412. Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Section 405, comprising section 303 of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, was editorially reclassified as section 3073 of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 406, comprising act June 24, 1948, ch. 632, 62 Stat. 648, was editorially reclassified as a note under section 3073 of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 407, comprising section 1602 of the Supplemental Appropriation Act, 1959, Pub. L. 85–766, was editorially reclassified as section 3328 of chapter 45 of this title.

Section 408, comprising section 201(d) of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, was editorially reclassified as section 3005 of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 409, comprising sections 205(c), 206(a), and 207(c) of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, was editorially reclassified as section 3004 of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 409a, comprising the National Security Agency Voluntary Separation Act, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, §301, and section 301 of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, was editorially reclassified as section 3071 of chapter 44 of this title.

Sections 409b to 412, comprising sections 302, 311(b), 308, 307, and 411, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3072, 3072a, 3075, 3074, and 3006, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
