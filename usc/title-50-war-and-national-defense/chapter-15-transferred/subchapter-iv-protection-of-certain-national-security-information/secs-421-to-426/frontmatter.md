### §§421 to 426. Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Sections 421 and 422, comprising sections 601 and 602, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3121 and 3122, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 423, comprising former section 603 of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, was editorially reclassified as section 3123 of chapter 44 of this title.

Sections 424 to 426, comprising sections 603 to 605, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3124 to 3126, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
