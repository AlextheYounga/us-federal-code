### §§413 to 415d. Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Sections 413 to 413b, comprising sections 501 to 503, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3091 to 3093, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 413c, comprising section 1079 of the National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2008, Pub. L. 110–181, was editorially reclassified as section 3307 of chapter 45 of this title.

Sections 414 to 415a–1, comprising sections 504 to 506A, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3094 to 3097, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 415a–2, comprising section 8107 of the Department of Defense Appropriations Act, 2009, Pub. L. 110–329, div. C, and also of the Consolidated Security, Disaster Assistance, and Continuing Appropriations Act, 2009, Pub. L. 110–329, was editorially reclassified as section 3305 of chapter 45 of this title.

Section 415a–3, comprising former section 8104 of the Department of Defense Appropriations Act, 2010, Pub. L. 111–118, was editorially reclassified as a note under section 3103 of chapter 44 of this title.

Sections 415a–4 to 415b, comprising sections 506B to 507, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3098 to 3106, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.

Section 415c, comprising section 601 of the Implementing Recommendations of the 9/11 Commission Act of 2007, Pub. L. 110–53, was editorially reclassified as section 3306 of chapter 45 of this title.

Section 415d, comprising section 508 of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, was editorially reclassified as section 3107 of chapter 44 of this title.
