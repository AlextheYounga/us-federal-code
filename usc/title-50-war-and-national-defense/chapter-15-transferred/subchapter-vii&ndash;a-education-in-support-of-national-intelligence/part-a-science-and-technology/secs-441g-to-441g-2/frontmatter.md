### §§441g to 441g–2. Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Sections 441g to 441g–2, comprising sections 1001 to 1003, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3191 to 3193, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
