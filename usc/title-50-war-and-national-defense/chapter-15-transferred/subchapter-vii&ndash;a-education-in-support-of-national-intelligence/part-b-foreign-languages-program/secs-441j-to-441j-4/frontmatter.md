### §§441j to 441j–4. Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Sections 441j to 441j–4, comprising sections 1011 to 1015, respectively, of the National Security Act of 1947, act July 26, 1947, ch. 343, were editorially reclassified as sections 3201 to 3205, respectively, of chapter 44 of this title.
