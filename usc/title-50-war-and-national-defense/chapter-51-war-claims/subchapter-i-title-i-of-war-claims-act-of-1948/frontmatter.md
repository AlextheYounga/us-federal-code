### SUBCHAPTER I—TITLE I OF WAR CLAIMS ACT OF 1948 ###

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 87–846, §101, Oct. 22, 1962, 76 Stat. 1107, designated sections 2 to 17 of the War Claims Act of 1948 (act July 3, 1948, ch. 826), as "title I" of the act without supplying a name for such title, which for purposes of codification has been set out as this subchapter.
