### SUBCHAPTER II—TITLE II OF WAR CLAIMS ACT OF 1948 ###

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 87–846, §103, Oct. 22, 1962, 76 Stat. 1107, added "title II" (§§201 to 217) to the War Claims Act of 1948 (act July 3, 1948, ch. 826) without supplying a name for such title, which for purposes of codification has been set out as this subchapter.
