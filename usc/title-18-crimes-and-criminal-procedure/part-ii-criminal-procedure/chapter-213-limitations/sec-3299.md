### §3299. Child abduction and sex offenses ###

Notwithstanding any other law, an indictment may be found or an information instituted at any time without limitation for any offense under section 1201 involving a minor victim, and for any felony under chapter 109A, 110 (except for section <sup><a href="#3299_1_target" name="3299_1">1</a></sup> 2257 and 2257A), or 117, or section 1591.

(Added Pub. L. 109–248, title II, §211(1), July 27, 2006, 120 Stat. 616.)

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be “sections”.](#3299_1)
