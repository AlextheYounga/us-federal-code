### §775. Payments nonassignable and exempt from process ###

No payment under sections 771 to 775 <sup><a href="#775_1_target" name="775_1">1</a></sup> of this title shall be assignable, either in law or in equity, or be subject to execution, levy, lien, attachment, garnishment, or other legal process.

(Aug. 19, 1950, ch. 761, §5, 64 Stat. 466.)
