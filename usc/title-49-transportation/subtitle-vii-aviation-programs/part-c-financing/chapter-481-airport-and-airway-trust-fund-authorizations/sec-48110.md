### §48110. Facilities for advanced training of maintenance technicians for air carrier aircraft ###

For the fiscal years ending September 30, 1993–1995, amounts necessary to carry out section 44515 <sup><a href="#48110_1_target" name="48110_1">1</a></sup> of this title may be appropriated to the Secretary of Transportation out of the Airport and Airway Trust Fund established under section 9502 of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 (26 U.S.C. 9502). The amounts remain available until expended.

(Pub. L. 103–272, §1(e), July 5, 1994, 108 Stat. 1297.)
