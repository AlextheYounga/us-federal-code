### §1445. Succession of Federal Home Loan Banks ###

Each Federal Home Loan Bank shall have succession until dissolved by the Director under this chapter or by further act of Congress.

([July 22, 1932, ch. 522, §25, 47 Stat. 740](/statviewer.htm?volume=47&page=740); [Pub. L. 101–73, title VII, §701(b)(1), (3)(A), Aug. 9, 1989, 103 Stat. 412](/statviewer.htm?volume=103&page=412); [Pub. L. 110–289, div. A, title II, §1204(8), July 30, 2008, 122 Stat. 2786](/statviewer.htm?volume=122&page=2786).)

#### Amendments ####

**2008**—Pub. L. 110–289 substituted "the Director" for "the Board".

**1989**—Pub. L. 101–73 substituted "Board" for "board".
