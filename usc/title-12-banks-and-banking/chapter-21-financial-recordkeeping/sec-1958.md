### §1958. Compliance ###

The Secretary shall have the responsibility to assure compliance with the requirements of this chapter and sections 1730d <sup><a href="#1958_1_target" name="1958_1">1</a></sup> and 1829b of this title and may delegate such responsibility to the appropriate bank supervisory agency, or other supervisory agency.

(Pub. L. 91–508, title I, §128, Oct. 26, 1970, 84 Stat. 1118.)

#### References in Text ####

Section 1730d of this title, referred to in text, was repealed by Pub. L. 101–73, title IV, §407, Aug. 9, 1989, 103 Stat. 363.
