### §§911 to 915. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 911, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §24, 39 Stat. 379](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=379); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, §4, 47 Stat. 13](/statviewer.htm?volume=47&page=13); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title III, §306(b), 69 Stat. 665](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=665); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(d), (h), 73 Stat. 386](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=386), [387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), provided for amount to be carried in reserve account by land bank associations. See section 2052 of this title.

Section 912, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §24, 39 Stat. 379](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=379); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, §4, 47 Stat. 13](/statviewer.htm?volume=47&page=13), covered making good of any impairment of reserve before payment of any dividends.

Section 913, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §24, 39 Stat. 379](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=379); [Jan. 23, 1932, ch. 9, §4, 47 Stat. 13](/statviewer.htm?volume=47&page=13); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, §4, 49 Stat. 315](/statviewer.htm?volume=49&page=315), provided for payment of dividends by land bank associations on balance of net earnings. See section 2052 of this title.

Section 914, act [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §24, 39 Stat. 379](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=379); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933, covered investment of reserves of land bank associations.

Section 915, act [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §24, 39 Stat. 379](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=379), provided for disposition of reserves on liquidation of land bank associations.
