### §§751 to 757. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 751, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(a), 39 Stat. 369](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=369); [Apr. 20, 1920, ch. 154, §2, 41 Stat. 570](/statviewer.htm?volume=41&page=570); [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, §2, 59 Stat. 266](/statviewer.htm?volume=59&page=266); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 384](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=384), covered investigation of applications for mortgage loans by the loan committee. See section 2033 of this title.

Section 752, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(b), 39 Stat. 369](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=369); [Apr. 20, 1920, ch. 154, §2, 41 Stat. 570](/statviewer.htm?volume=41&page=570); [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, §2, 59 Stat. 266](/statviewer.htm?volume=59&page=266); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 385](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=385), covered submission of loan applications and required reports to land banks. See section 2033 of this title.

Section 753, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(c), 39 Stat. 369](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=369); [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, §2, 59 Stat. 266](/statviewer.htm?volume=59&page=266); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 385](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=385), provided for form for appraisal reports.

Section 754, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(d), 39 Stat. 369](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=369); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 385](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=385), dealt with conflicting interests of farm credit or land bank appraisers, committeemen, and association directors. See section 2254 of this title.

Section 755, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(e), 39 Stat. 369](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=369); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 385](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=385), provided for appraisal standards studies by Federal land banks. See section 2033 of this title.

Section 756, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(f), 39 Stat. 369](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=369); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 385](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=385), covered appraisals, reports, and standards by farm credit appraisers. See section 2012 of this title.

Section 757, act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §10(g), as added [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title III, §303, 69 Stat. 664](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=664); amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(b), 73 Stat. 385](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=385), set out duties of farm credit appraisers. See section 2254 of this title.
