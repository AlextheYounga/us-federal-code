### §§741 to 747. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 741, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9, 39 Stat. 368](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=368); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), covered members' rights to loans. See section 2201 of this title.

Section 742, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9, 39 Stat. 368](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=368); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §102(a), 73 Stat. 384](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=384), covered payments for stock from proceeds of member's mortgage loan. See section 2033 of this title.

Section 743, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9, 39 Stat. 368](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=368); Ex. Ord. No. 6084, Mar. 27, 1933; Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), authorized retention of commissions on interest payments. See section 2033 of this title.

Section 744, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9, 39 Stat. 368](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=368); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), provided for individual liability of shareholders.

Section 744a, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VII, §72, 48 Stat. 271](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=271); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), relieved shareholders of land bank associations of personal liability for contracts, debts, and engagements entered into after June 16, 1933.

Section 745, acts [July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9, 39 Stat. 368](/statviewer.htm?volume=39&page=368); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, 20, 49 Stat. 319](/statviewer.htm?volume=49&page=319); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §24, 50 Stat. 710](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=710); [June 30, 1945, ch. 204, §1(b), 59 Stat. 266](/statviewer.htm?volume=59&page=266); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), (i), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), provided for new members for land bank associations. See section 2033 of this title.

Section 746, act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9 (par.) as added [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §25(a), 50 Stat. 710](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=710); amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), provided for a common board of directors for two or more associations. See section 2032 of this title.

Section 747, act July 17, 1916, ch. 245, title I, §9 (par.), as added [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §25(a), 50 Stat. 711](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=711); amended Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), provided for transfer of powers to a loan committee. See section 2033 of this title.
