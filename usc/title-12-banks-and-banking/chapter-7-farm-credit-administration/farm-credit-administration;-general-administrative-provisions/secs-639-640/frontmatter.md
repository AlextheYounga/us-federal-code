### §§639, 640. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 639, act [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VIII, §82, 48 Stat. 273](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=273), made a supplementary grant of powers to Governor of Farm Credit Administration.

Section 640, act [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VIII, §85, 48 Stat. 273](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=273), authorized Farm Credit Administration to have a seal. See section 2248 of this title.
