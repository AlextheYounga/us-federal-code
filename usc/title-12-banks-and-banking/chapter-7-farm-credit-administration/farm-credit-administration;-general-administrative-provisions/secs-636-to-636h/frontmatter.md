### §§636 to 636h. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 636, acts [May 12, 1933, ch. 25, title II, §40, 48 Stat. 51](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=51); Sept. 6, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–554, §8(a), 80 Stat. 648](/statviewer.htm?volume=80&page=648), provided for organization of Farm Credit Administration. See section 2247 of this title.

Section 636a, act [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §2, 67 Stat. 390](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=390), stated Congressional declaration of policy concerning agricultural credit. See section 2001 of this title.

Section 636a note, act [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §1, 67 Stat. 390](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=390), provided that such act Aug. 6, 1953, should be known as "Farm Credit Act of 1953".

Section 636b, act [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §3, 67 Stat. 390](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=390), covered the creation of Farm Credit Administration as an independent agency in executive branch. See section 2241 et seq. of this title.

Section 636c, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §4, 67 Stat. 390](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=390); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title IV, §402, 69 Stat. 666](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=666); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387); Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525, §6, 80 Stat. 335](/statviewer.htm?volume=80&page=335), provided for creation of Federal Farm Credit Board. See section 2242 et seq. of this title.

Section 636d, act [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §5, 67 Stat. 392](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=392); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(j), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387); Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–367, title III, §302(a), 75 Stat. 793](/statviewer.htm?volume=75&page=793), provided for position of Governor of Farm Credit Administration. See section 2244 et seq. of this title.

Section 636e, act [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §6, 67 Stat. 393](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=393), covered duties of Federal Farm Credit Board. See section 2227 of this title.

Section 636f, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §7, 67 Stat. 393](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=393); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §110(c), 69 Stat. 662](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=662); Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–353, §3(n), 75 Stat. 774](/statviewer.htm?volume=75&page=774), provided for abolishment of certain offices and funds under program as it existed prior to 1953.

Section 636g, acts [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §8, 67 Stat. 394](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=394); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §107(a), 70 Stat. 666](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=666); Aug. 18, 1959, [Pub. L. 86–168, title I, §104(h), 73 Stat. 387](/statviewer.htm?volume=73&page=387), authorized delegation of powers to institutions in farm credit districts. See section 2253 of this title.

Section 636h, act [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §17(b), 67 Stat. 399](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=399), covered administrative expenditures of Farm Credit Administration. See section 2249 of this title.
