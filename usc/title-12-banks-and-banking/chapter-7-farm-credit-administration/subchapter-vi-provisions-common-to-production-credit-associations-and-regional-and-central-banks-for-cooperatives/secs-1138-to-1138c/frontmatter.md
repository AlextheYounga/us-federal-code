### §§1138 to 1138c. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 1138, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VI, §60, 48 Stat. 266](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=266); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §5(a), 50 Stat. 704](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=704); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(l), 70 Stat. 666](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=666), set out general corporate powers common to banks for cooperatives and production credit associations. See sections 2093 and 2122 of this title.

Section 1138a, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VI, §61, 48 Stat. 267](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=267); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(m), 70 Stat. 666](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=666), provided for examination of parts of farm credit system by examiners of Farm Credit Administration. See section 2254 of this title.

Section 1138b, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VI, §62, 48 Stat. 267](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=267); [Jan. 31, 1934, ch. 7, §11, 48 Stat. 347](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=347); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(n), 70 Stat. 666](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=666); Oct. 4, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–353, §3(j), 75 Stat. 774](/statviewer.htm?volume=75&page=774), gave designated parts of farm credit system authority to act as fiscal agents of United States.

Section 1138c, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title VI, §63, 48 Stat. 267](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=267), [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title II, §205, 69 Stat. 663](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=663); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(o), 70 Stat. 666](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=666), provided that obligations of banks for cooperatives and production credit associations be deemed instrumentalities of United States and provided for termination of tax exemption after retirement of government-owned stock. See sections 2098 and 2134 of this title.
