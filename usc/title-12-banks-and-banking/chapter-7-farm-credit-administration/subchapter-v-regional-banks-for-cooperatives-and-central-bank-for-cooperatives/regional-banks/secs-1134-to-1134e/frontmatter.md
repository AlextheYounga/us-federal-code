### §§1134 to 1134e. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 1134, acts June 16, 1933, ch. 98 title I, §2, 48 Stat. 257; [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §11, 50 Stat. 708](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=708); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(a), 70 Stat. 664](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=664), authorized creation of regional banks for cooperatives and establishment of boards of directors of such banks. See section 2121 of this title.

Section 1134a, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title I, §3, 48 Stat. 257](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=257); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(b), 70 Stat. 665](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=665), provided for charters and bylaws for banks for cooperatives. See section 2121 of this title.

Section 1134b, act [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title IV, §40, 48 Stat. 264](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=264), provided for capital stock of banks for cooperatives and its amounts, value, and payments. See section 2124 of this title.

Section 1134c, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title IV, §41, 48 Stat. 264](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=264); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, §14, 49 Stat. 317](/statviewer.htm?volume=49&page=317); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §36, 50 Stat. 717](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=717); [Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792, title IV, §417(a), 63 Stat. 1058](/statviewer.htm?volume=63&page=1058); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §107, 69 Stat. 662](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=662); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(k), 70 Stat. 665](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=665); Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525, §4(a), 80 Stat. 334](/statviewer.htm?volume=80&page=334); Dec. 15, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–204, §3(b), 81 Stat. 612](/statviewer.htm?volume=81&page=612), covered lending powers of banks for cooperatives. See section 2122 of this title.

Section 1134d, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title IV, §42, 48 Stat. 264](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=264); [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §13, 67 Stat. 396](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=396); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §101, 69 Stat. 655](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=655), provided for stock of banks for cooperatives. See section 2124 of this title.

Section 1134e, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title IV, §43, 48 Stat. 265](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=265); Oct. 17, 1968, [Pub. L. 90–582, §3, 82 Stat. 1145](/statviewer.htm?volume=82&page=1145), provided for retirement of stock in banks for cooperatives held by the government. See section 2126 of this title.
