### §§1134f to 1134m. Repealed. [Pub. L. 92–181, title V, §5.26(a), Dec. 10, 1971, 85 Stat. 624](/statviewer.htm?volume=85&page=624) ###

Section 1134f, act [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §30, 48 Stat. 261](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=261), authorized the creation of a central bank for cooperatives. See section 2121 of this title.

Section 1134g, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §31, 48 Stat. 262](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=262); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §104, 69 Stat. 659](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=659); June 11, 1960, [Pub. L. 86–503, §1 (part), 74 Stat. 197](/statviewer.htm?volume=74&page=197), provided for a board of directors for central bank for cooperatives. See section 2123 of this title.

Section 1134h, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §32, 48 Stat. 262](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=262); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §105, 69 Stat. 661](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=661), set out powers of board of directors for central bank for cooperative. See section 2123 of this title.

Section 1134i, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §33, 48 Stat. 262](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=262); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §108(a), 69 Stat. 662](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=662), provided for capital stock of central bank for cooperatives. See section 2124 of this title.

Section 1134j, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §§34, 38, 48 Stat. 262](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=262), [264](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=264); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, §13, 49 Stat. 317](/statviewer.htm?volume=49&page=317); [Aug. 19, 1937, ch. 704, §35, 50 Stat. 716](/statviewer.htm?volume=50&page=716); [Oct. 31, 1949, ch. 792, title IV, §417(b), 63 Stat. 1059](/statviewer.htm?volume=63&page=1059); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §§106, 108(b), 69 Stat. 661](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=661), [662](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=662); [July 26, 1956, ch. 741, title I, §105(j), 70 Stat. 665](/statviewer.htm?volume=70&page=665); Aug. 2, 1966, [Pub. L. 89–525, §4(a), 80 Stat. 334](/statviewer.htm?volume=80&page=334); Dec. 15, 1967, [Pub. L. 90–204, §3(b), 81 Stat. 612](/statviewer.htm?volume=81&page=612), authorized central bank to make loans and provided for prevention of duplication of effort on part of central bank and banks for cooperatives. See section 2122 of this title.

Section 1134k, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §35, 48 Stat. 263](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=263); [June 3, 1935, ch. 164, §15, 49 Stat. 318](/statviewer.htm?volume=49&page=318); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §102, 69 Stat. 658](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=658), made regional bank stock provisions applicable to ownership of stock by central bank borrowers and provided for payments for such stock. See section 2130 of this title.

Section 1134l, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §36, 48 Stat. 263](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=263); [Aug. 6, 1953, ch. 335, §12, 67 Stat. 395](/statviewer.htm?volume=67&page=395); [Aug. 11, 1955, ch. 785, title I, §103(a), 69 Stat. 658](/statviewer.htm?volume=69&page=658); Oct. 3, 1961, [Pub. L. 87–343, §2(3), 75 Stat. 758](/statviewer.htm?volume=75&page=758); Aug. 31, 1964, [Pub. L. 88–528, 78 Stat. 736](/statviewer.htm?volume=78&page=736), provided for disposition of earnings and maintenance of reserves of central bank. See section 2132 of this title.

Section 1134m, acts [June 16, 1933, ch. 98, title III, §37, 48 Stat. 263](/statviewer.htm?volume=48&page=263); [Aug. 23, 1954, ch. 834, §1, 68 Stat. 770](/statviewer.htm?volume=68&page=770), covered debentures, amount of such debentures, security, preparation, and issuance of such debentures, and consolidated debentures of central and regional banks for cooperatives. See section 2153 of this title.
