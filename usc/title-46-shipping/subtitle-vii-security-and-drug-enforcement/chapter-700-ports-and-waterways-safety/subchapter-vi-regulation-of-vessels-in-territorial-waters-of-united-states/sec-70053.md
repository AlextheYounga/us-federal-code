### §70053. Enforcement provisions ###

The President may employ such departments, agencies, officers, or instrumentalities of the United States as he may deem necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Added Pub. L. 115–282, title IV, §407(d)(3), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4267.)

#### Codification ####

Section, as added by Pub. L. 115–282, is based on act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, title II, §4, 40 Stat. 220, which was formerly classified to section 194 of Title 50, War and National Defense, before being transferred to this chapter and renumbered as this section.

#### Amendments ####

**2018**—Pub. L. 115–282 transferred section 4 of title II of act June 15, 1917, ch. 30, to this chapter. See Codification note above.
