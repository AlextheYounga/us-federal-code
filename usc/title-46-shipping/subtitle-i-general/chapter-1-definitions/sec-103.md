### §103. Boundary Line ###

In this title, the term “Boundary Line” means a line established under section 2(b) of the Act of February 19, 1895 (33 U.S.C. 151).<sup><a href="#103_1_target" name="103_1">1</a></sup>

(Pub. L. 109–304, §4, Oct. 6, 2006, 120 Stat. 1486.)
