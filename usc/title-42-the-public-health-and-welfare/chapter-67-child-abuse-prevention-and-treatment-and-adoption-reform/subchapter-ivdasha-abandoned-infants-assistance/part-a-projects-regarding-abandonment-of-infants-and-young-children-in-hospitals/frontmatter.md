### Part A—Projects Regarding Abandonment of Infants and Young Children in Hospitals ###

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 102–236, §7, Dec. 12, 1991, 105 Stat. 1816, substituted "Projects Regarding Abandonment of Infants and Young Children in Hospitals" for "Foster Care and Residential Care of Infants and Young Children Abandoned in Hospitals" in part heading.
