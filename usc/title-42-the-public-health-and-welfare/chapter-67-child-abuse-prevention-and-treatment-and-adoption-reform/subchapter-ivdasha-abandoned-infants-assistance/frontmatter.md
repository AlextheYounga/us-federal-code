### SUBCHAPTER IV–A—ABANDONED INFANTS ASSISTANCE ###

#### Codification ####

This subchapter was comprised generally of Pub. L. 100–505, Oct. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 2533, prior to repeal by Pub. L. 115–271, title VII, §7065(b), Oct. 24, 2018, 132 Stat. 4028. Pub. L. 100–505 was formerly set out as a note under section 670 of this title. Section 105 of Pub. L. 100–505, which provided for termination of the grant program described in this subchapter on Sept. 30, 1991, was repealed by Pub. L. 102–236, §8, Dec. 12, 1991, 105 Stat. 1816.
