### Part C—Admission to Hospitals and Outpatient Facilities; Confidentiality of Records ###

#### Codification ####

Part consists of part C and portions of part D of title III of Pub. L. 91–616. Part B of such title enacted section 2688j–2 of this title. Part D, in addition to enacting section 4582 of this title, amended sections 246 and 2688h of this title.
