### SUBCHAPTER V—MATERNAL AND CHILD HEALTH SERVICES BLOCK GRANT ###

#### Amendments ####

**1981**—Pub. L. 97–35, title XXI, §2192(a), Aug. 13, 1981, 95 Stat. 818, substituted "MATERNAL AND CHILD HEALTH SERVICES BLOCK GRANT" for "MATERNAL AND CHILD HEALTH AND CRIPPLED CHILDREN'S SERVICES" as the heading of title V of the Social Security Act [42 U.S.C. 701 et seq.] as part of the general revision of this subchapter.
