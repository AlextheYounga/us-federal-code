### Part E—Federal Payments for Foster Care, Prevention, and Permanency ###

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 115–123, div. E, title VII, §50733(a), Feb. 9, 2018, 132 Stat. 252, substituted "Federal Payments for Foster Care, Prevention, and Permanency" for "Federal Payments for Foster Care and Adoption Assistance" in part heading.
