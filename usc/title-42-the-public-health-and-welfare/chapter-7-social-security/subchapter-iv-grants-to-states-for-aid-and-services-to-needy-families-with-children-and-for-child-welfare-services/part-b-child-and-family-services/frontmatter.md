### Part B—Child and Family Services ###

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 103–66, title XIII, §13711(a)(1), Aug. 10, 1993, 107 Stat. 649, substituted "Child and Family Services" for "Child Welfare Services" in part B heading.

Pub. L. 90–248, title II, §240(c), Jan. 2, 1968, 81 Stat. 911, added part B heading.
