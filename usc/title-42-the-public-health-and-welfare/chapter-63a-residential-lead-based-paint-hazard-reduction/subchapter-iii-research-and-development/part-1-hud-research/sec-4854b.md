### §4854b. Authorization ###

Of the total amount approved in appropriation Acts under section 4852(o) <sup><a href="#4854b_1_target" name="4854b_1">1</a></sup> of this title, there shall be set aside to carry out this part $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1993, and $5,000,000 for fiscal year 1994.

(Pub. L. 102–550, title X, §1053, Oct. 28, 1992, 106 Stat. 3926.)

#### References in Text ####

Section 4852(o) of this title, referred to in text, was redesignated section 4852(p) of this title by Pub. L. 103–233, title III, §305(a)(1), Apr. 11, 1994, 108 Stat. 370.
