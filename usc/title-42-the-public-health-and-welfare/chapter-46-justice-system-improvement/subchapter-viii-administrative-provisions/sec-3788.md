### §3788. Transferred ###

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

Section 3788 was editorially reclassified as section 10226 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior section 3788, Pub. L. 90–351, title I, §810, as added Pub. L. 96–157, §2, Dec. 27, 1979, 93 Stat. 1204, contained in part provisions similar to subsec. (a) of section 10226 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement, prior to repeal by section 609B(e) of Pub. L. 98–473.
