### §3797dd–1. Omitted ###

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

L. 110–199, title I, §114(2), Apr. 9, 2008, 122 Stat. 677, which authorized appropriations for fiscal years 2009 and 2010, was omitted as obsolete and was subsequently repealed by Pub.
