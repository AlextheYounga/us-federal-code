### §3796. Transferred ###

#### Codification ####

Section 3796 was editorially reclassified as section 10281 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior section 3796, Pub. L. 90–351, title I, §701, as added Pub. L. 94–430, §2, Sept. 29, 1976, 90 Stat. 1346, contained provisions similar to those in section 10281 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement, prior to the general amendment of title I of Pub. L. 90–351 by Pub. L. 96–157.
