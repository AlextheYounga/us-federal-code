### Part J—Privacy Protections for Victims of Domestic Violence, Dating Violence, Sexual Violence, and Stalking ###

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

This part was, in the original, subtitle K of title IV of Pub. L. 103–322, as added by Pub. L. 109–162, and was redesignated as part J of this subchapter for purposes of codification.
