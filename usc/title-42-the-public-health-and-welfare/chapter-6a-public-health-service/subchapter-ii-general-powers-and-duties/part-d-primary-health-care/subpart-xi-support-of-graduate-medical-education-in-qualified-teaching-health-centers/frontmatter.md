### subpart xi—support of graduate medical education in qualified teaching health centers ###

#### Codification ####

Subpart is comprised of subpart XI of part D of title III of act July 1, 1944. Another subpart XI of part D of title III of the Act was redesignated subpart XII by Pub. L. 115–63, title III, §301(c)(1), Sept. 29, 2017, 131 Stat. 1172, and is classified to subpart XII (§256i) of this part.
