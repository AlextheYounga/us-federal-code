### subpart ii—block grants for substance use prevention, treatment, and recovery services ###

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 117–328, div. FF, title I, §1241(a)(2), Dec. 29, 2022, 136 Stat. 5677, amended subpart heading generally. Prior to amendment, subpart heading read as follows: "Block Grants for Prevention and Treatment of Substance Abuse".
