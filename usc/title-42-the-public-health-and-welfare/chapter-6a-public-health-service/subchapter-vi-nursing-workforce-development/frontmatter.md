### SUBCHAPTER VI—NURSING WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT ###

#### Amendments ####

**1998**—Pub. L. 105–392, title I, §123(1), Nov. 13, 1998, 112 Stat. 3562, added subchapter VI heading and struck out former subchapter VI heading “NURSE EDUCATION”.

**1985**—Pub. L. 99–92, §9(b)(3), Aug. 16, 1985, 99 Stat. 400, substituted “NURSE EDUCATION” for “NURSE TRAINING” in subchapter VI heading.
