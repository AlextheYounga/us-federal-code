### Part F—National Advisory Council on Nurse Education and Practice ###

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior part F, consisting of section 297q, was redesignated part I (§298d) of this subchapter.

#### Amendments ####

**2010**—Pub. L. 111–148, title V, §5310(b)(8)(B), Mar. 23, 2010, 124 Stat. 631, redesignated part G “National Advisory Council on Nurse Education and Practice” as F.
