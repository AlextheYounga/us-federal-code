### Part G—Public Service Announcements ###

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior part G, consisting of section 297t, was redesignated part F of this subchapter.

#### Amendments ####

**2010**—Pub. L. 111–148, title V, §5310(b)(9)(B), Mar. 23, 2010, 124 Stat. 631, redesignated part H "Public Service Announcements" as G.
