### subpart 1—general reform ###

#### Amendments ####

**2010**—Pub. L. 111–148, title I, §§1201(1), 1255, formerly §1253, title X, §10103(e), (f)(1), Mar. 23, 2010, 124 Stat. 154, 162, 895, substituted “general reform” for “portability, access, and renewability requirements” in subpart heading, effective for plan years beginning on or after Jan. 1, 2014.
