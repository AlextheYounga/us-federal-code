### Part A—Genetic Diseases ###

#### Codification ####

Pub. L. 94–278, title IV, §403(a), Apr. 22, 1976, 90 Stat. 407, substituted "Genetic Diseases" for "Sickle Cell Anemia Programs" in part A heading.

Pub. L. 92–414, §4(1), Aug. 29, 1972, 86 Stat. 652, redesignated subchapter IX heading as part A heading and substituted "Sickle Cell Anemia Programs" for "Sickle Cell Anemia Program".
