### Part E—Sexual Abuse Prevention Program ###

#### Prior Provisions ####

A prior part E, consisting of sections 5714a and 5714b, was redesignated part F by Pub. L. 106–71, §3(n)(1)(B), Oct. 12, 1999, 113 Stat. 1040.

#### Amendments ####

**1999**—Pub. L. 106–71, §3(n)(1)(C), Oct. 12, 1999, 113 Stat. 1040, added part heading. Former part E redesignated F.
