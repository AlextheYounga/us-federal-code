### §5655. Transferred ###

Section 5655 was editorially reclassified as section 11145 of Title 34, Crime Control and Law Enforcement.

L. 98–473, title II, §§634, 670(a), Oct. 12, 1984, 98 Stat. 2119, 2129, effective Oct. 12, 1984.
