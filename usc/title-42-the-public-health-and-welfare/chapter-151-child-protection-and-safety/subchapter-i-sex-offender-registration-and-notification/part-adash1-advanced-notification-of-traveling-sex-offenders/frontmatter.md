### Part A–1—Advanced Notification of Traveling Sex Offenders ###

#### Codification ####

Part was enacted as part of the International Megan's Law to Prevent Child Exploitation and Other Sexual Crimes Through Advanced Notification of Traveling Sex Offenders, and not as part of the Sex Offender Registration and Notification Act which comprises this subchapter or the Adam Walsh Child Protection and Safety Act of 2006 which comprises this chapter.
