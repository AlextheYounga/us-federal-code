### SUBCHAPTER XXXV—LOAN REPAYMENT FOR PROSECUTORS AND PUBLIC DEFENDERS ###

#### Codification ####

This subchapter is comprised of part JJ of title I of the Omnibus Crime Control and Safe Streets Act of 1968, Pub. L. 90–351. Another part JJ of title I of Pub. L. 90–351 is classified to subchapter XXXVI (§10681) of this chapter.
