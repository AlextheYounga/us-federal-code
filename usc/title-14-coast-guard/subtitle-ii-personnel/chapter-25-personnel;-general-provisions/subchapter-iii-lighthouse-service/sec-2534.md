### §2534. Application for benefits ###

(a) Application for the benefits of section 2533 shall be made in such manner and form as the Civil Service Commission <sup><a href="#2534_1_target" name="2534_1">1</a></sup> shall prescribe.

(b) The Civil Service Commission <sup><a href="#2534_1_target" name="2534_1">1</a></sup> shall perform, or cause to be performed, such acts, and shall make such rules and regulations, as may be necessary or proper to carry out section 2533.

(c) That <sup><a href="#2534_2_target" name="2534_2">2</a></sup> annuities authorized by section 2533 may hereafter be paid out of the Civil Service Retirement and Disability Fund.

(Added and amended Pub. L. 116–283, div. G, title LVXXXV [LXXXV], §8511(b), Jan. 1, 2021, 134 Stat. 4758.)
