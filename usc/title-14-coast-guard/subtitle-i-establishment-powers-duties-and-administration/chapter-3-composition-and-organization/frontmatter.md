L. 115–282, title I, §104(a), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4196.

#### Amendments ####

**2018**—Pub. L. 115–282, title I, §104(a), title III, §304(c)(1), Dec. 4, 2018, 132 Stat. 4196, 4245, inserted chapter 3 designation and heading and added items 301 to 319.
