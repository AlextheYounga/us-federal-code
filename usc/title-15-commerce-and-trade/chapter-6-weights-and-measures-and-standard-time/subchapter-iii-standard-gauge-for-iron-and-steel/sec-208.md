### §208. Variations ###

In the practical use and application of the standard gauge esablished <sup><a href="#208_1_target" name="208_1">1</a></sup> in section 206 of this title a variation of 2½ percent, either way may be allowed.

(Mar. 3, 1893, ch. 221, §3, 27 Stat. 746.)

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be "established".](#208_1)
