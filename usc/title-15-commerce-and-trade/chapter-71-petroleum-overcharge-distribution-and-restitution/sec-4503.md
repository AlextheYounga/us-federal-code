### §4503. Deposit of remainder of excess amount into Treasury as indirect restitution ###

The amount that remains from the excess amount described in section 4502(c) <sup><a href="#4503_1_target" name="4503_1">1</a></sup> of this title after all disbursements have been made for a fiscal year under section 4502(d) <sup><a href="#4503_1_target" name="4503_1">1</a></sup> of this title shall be deposited by the Secretary of the Treasury into the general fund of the Treasury.

(Pub. L. 99–509, title III, §3004, Oct. 21, 1986, 100 Stat. 1884.)

#### **Editorial Notes** ####

#### References in Text ####

Section 4502(c) and (d) of this title, referred to in text, was repealed by section 4502(e) of this title.
