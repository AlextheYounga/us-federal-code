### §2447e. Definition of weapon system component ###

In this subchapter, the term "weapon system component" has the meaning given the term "major system component" in section 2446a of this title.

(Added Pub. L. 114–328, div. A, title VIII, §806(a)(1), Dec. 23, 2016, 130 Stat. 2259.)

#### Effective Date ####

Section effective Jan. 1, 2017, see section 806(a)(2) of Pub. L. 114–328, set out as a note under section 2447a of this title.
