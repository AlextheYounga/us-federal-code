Sec.

601. Positions of importance and responsibility: generals and lieutenant generals; admirals and vice admirals.

#### Amendments ####

**2018**—Pub. L. 115–232, div. A, title V, §503(a)(2), Aug. 13, 2018, 132 Stat. 1742, added item 605.
