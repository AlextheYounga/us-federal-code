### §1101. Omitted ###

#### Codification ####

Section, Pub. L. 106–553, §1(a)(2) [title X, §1002], Dec. 21, 2000, 114 Stat. 2762, 2762A–128, related to the purpose of this chapter, to facilitate access to signals of local television stations for households in nonserved areas and underserved areas.

#### Short Title ####

Pub. L. 106–553, §1(a)(2) [title X, §1001], Dec. 21, 2000, 114 Stat. 2762, 2762A–128, provided that title X of H.R. 5548, as enacted by section 1(a)(2) of Pub. L. 106–553, which enacted this chapter and amended section 339 of this title, could be cited as the "Launching Our Communities' Access to Local Television Act of 2000".
