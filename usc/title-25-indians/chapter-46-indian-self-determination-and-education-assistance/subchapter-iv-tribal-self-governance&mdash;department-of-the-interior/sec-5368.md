### §5368. Authorization of appropriations ###

There are authorized to be appropriated such sums as may be necessary to carry out this subchapter.

(Pub. L. 93–638, title IV, §408, as added Pub. L. 103–413, title II, §204, Oct. 25, 1994, 108 Stat. 4278.)

#### Codification ####

Section was formerly classified to section 458hh of this title prior to editorial reclassification and renumbering as this section.
