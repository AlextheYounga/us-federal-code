### §1300i–7. Hoopa Valley Tribe; confirmation of status ###

The existing govening <sup><a href="#1300i-7_1_target" name="1300i-7_1">1</a></sup> documents of the Hoopa Valley Tribe and the governing body established and elected thereunder, as heretofore recognized by the Secretary, are hereby ratified and confirmed.

(Pub. L. 100–580, §8, Oct. 31, 1988, 102 Stat. 2932.)

[<sup>1</sup> So in original. Probably should be "governing".](#1300i-7_1)
