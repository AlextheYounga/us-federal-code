### Part E—Tribal Self-Governance—Indian Health Service ###

#### Codification ####

This part is comprised of title V of Pub. L. 93–638, as added by Pub. L. 106–260, §4, Aug. 18, 2000, 114 Stat. 712. A former title V of Pub. L. 93–638 was added by Pub. L. 106–568, title XIII, §1302, Dec. 27, 2000, 114 Stat. 2936, was redesignated title VIII, and is classified to part H (§458ddd et seq.) of this subchapter.
